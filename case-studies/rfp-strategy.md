# RFP Strategy

## Principle

An RFP response should make the buyer feel safer, not just more informed.

## Response Structure

1. Clarify the buyer's real risk.
2. Show why the proposed solution fits that risk.
3. Make implementation feel credible.
4. Demonstrate measurement and accountability.

## Common Mistake

Many responses read like feature catalogs. Strong responses read like outcome plans.

## Practical Test

If a non-technical stakeholder cannot explain why your proposal is credible after reading the executive summary, the response is not ready.
