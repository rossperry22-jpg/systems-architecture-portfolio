# Systems Thinking

## Definition

Systems thinking means treating tools, people, process, and decisions as one connected operating environment.

## Practical Questions

- Where is friction introduced?
- Where is ownership unclear?
- Where does context get lost?
- Which parts should be standardized?
- Which parts should stay human-led?

## Architecture Standard

Good architecture is not the most complex design. It is the clearest design that can scale without falling apart under real operating pressure.
