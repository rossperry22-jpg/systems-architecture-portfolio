# System Flow Notes

The portfolio includes a simplified SVG at [`../../assets/images/architecture/hpe-workflow.svg`](../../assets/images/architecture/hpe-workflow.svg).

## Reading The Diagram

The diagram is intentionally simple:

- three workflow systems on the left
- one shared operating layer in the middle
- business outcomes on the right

That shape reflects the real point of the work. The technical challenge was less about any one platform and more about creating an operating layer that could unify them without hiding the process from the user.
